openBosnianaOS

Free, lightweight, and complete operating system!

  • An open-source operating system built on the Linux kernel and the GNU Project.
  • Designed for ease of use, with a lightweight and user-friendly desktop environment that works seamlessly even on older hardware.
  • Access thousands of completely free applications, ready to download and install via the application manager.

Features

Simple and Easy

Simply download one of the versions, create a bootable USB flash drive, and you're ready to go. It is designed to work 'out of the box' and comes fully equipped with the software and applications that most people need.

  • Office

    With the complete LibreOffice office suite, you can use word processing programs, create presentations, drawings, spreadsheets, or even databases. LibreOffice is compatible with Microsoft Office, meaning you can open and use all your existing Microsoft Office files.


  • Multimedia

    Enjoy music, watch TV and movies, or listen to radio online. We include modern and popular players that handle a wide range of codecs, so you don't have to search for them.



  • Developer-Friendly

    OpenBosniana OS is tailored for developers, integrating popular programming languages, compilers, interpreters, libraries, and development frameworks. Whether pre-installed or just a command away in our software repository, it streamlines and accelerates your development process.


  • Design

    Whether you're watching or editing your favorite files, OpenBosniana OS provides you with the tools you need to turn them into something special. Work in 3D with Blender, draw or edit images in GIMP, and use Inkscape for vector graphics.

    • Features

    Privacy

    OpenBosniana OS ensures your data is handled safely and reliably. The default web browser is a modified version of Firefox to make it harder for websites to collect user data. Tracking and telemetry features are disabled by default. With our built-in tools, in addition to preserving online privacy, you can also encrypt your home partition during installation—or other storage devices later on.




    Features

    Safety

    All Linux distributions are inherently safe. Every user has his own, protected part of the system in which work is done. OpenBosniana OS has numerous pre-configured tools and features to protect security and privacy. As long as you install software from secure repositories, you don't have to worry about unauthorized actions happening to your system.


  • Developer-Friendly

    With our built-in applications such as OB Welcome and OB Control Center, easily maintain, customize, and improve your system. Install the system once and update as needed.
