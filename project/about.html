ABOUT

What is OpenBosniana OS?

OpenBosniana OS is a free, user-friendly GNU/Linux-based operating system designed for desktops and laptops.

It is distributed as a LiveCD/DVD/USB ISO image and can also be installed on your computer. OpenBosniana OS prioritizes security, protecting you from viruses, adware, malware, and trojans, ensuring a safe computing experience.

With modern KDE Plasma as its default desktop environment, OpenBosniana OS also offers XFCE and MATE options for users who prefer lighter interfaces.

Fully customizable versions are available, making it ideal for use on USB drives.

Software updates and new applications are primarily sourced from Debian repositories, while a dedicated OpenBosniana OS repository provides updates for its specialized applications.

To learn more about the software sources, visit OpenBosniana OS Repositories.

OpenBosniana OS Main Focus

OpenBosniana OS is committed to delivering a secure, elegant, and out-of-the-box experience for its users.

Its design emphasizes simplicity and clarity, offering a clean and intuitive interface.

Packed with privacy-focused tools, multimedia applications, and internet utilities, OpenBosniana OS caters to both casual and advanced users.

Privacy is a core principle, with each version equipped with the best free tools for safeguarding your personal data.


What can I do with OpenBosniana OS?

Everything you do with other operating systems can be done with OpenBosniana OS—and more!

  • Internet Tools: Browse the web, send and receive emails, chat, blog, tweet, and stream videos online with a full suite of internet applications.
  • Photo Management: Edit photos, upload, and organize your photo collections using GIMP and other tools.
  • Music and Video: Listen to and manage your music collection, sync your MP3 player, stream internet radio, watch YouTube, and play TV shows, movies, and DVDs. You can even design DVD slideshows and much more.
  • Productivity: Boost your productivity with LibreOffice, allowing you to create and edit documents, presentations, spreadsheets, and graphs. LibreOffice is available in multiple languages and easily installed with the LibreOffice Manager.

OpenBosniana OS blends ease of use, adaptability, and security to provide a versatile operating system for everyone—from students and professionals to developers and home users.
