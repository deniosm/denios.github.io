FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)


Here’s a list of common questions and answers to help you better understand OpenBosniana OS and its features.

General Questions

Q: What is OpenBosniana OS?

A: OpenBosniana OS is a free, Linux-based operating system designed for desktops, laptops, and USB drives. It provides a secure, user-friendly experience with a focus on privacy, efficiency, and adaptability.

Q: Who can use OpenBosniana OS?

A: OpenBosniana OS is suitable for everyone—from beginners to advanced users. It’s ideal for students, educators, professionals, developers, and home users.

Q: Is OpenBosniana OS free?

A: Yes, OpenBosniana OS is completely free to download, use, and modify.

Q: What desktop environments does OpenBosniana OS offer?

A: OpenBosniana OS is available with KDE Plasma, XFCE, and MATE desktop environments.


Installation

Q: How do I install OpenBosniana OS?

A: OpenBosniana OS can be installed from a LiveCD, DVD, or USB ISO image. Simply boot your system from the media and follow the guided installation process.

Q: What are the system requirements?

A: OpenBosniana OS requires at least:

  • 2 GB of RAM (4 GB recommended)
  • 20 GB of free disk space
  • A 64-bit processor
  • USB or DVD drive for installation


Features and Usage

Q: What software comes pre-installed?

A: OpenBosniana OS includes internet browsers, email clients, office productivity tools (LibreOffice), multimedia applications, photo editors, and privacy-focused utilities.

Q: Can I install additional software?

A: Yes, you can install additional software using the built-in package manager, which accesses Debian repositories and the OpenBosniana OS repository.

Q: Is OpenBosniana OS secure?

A: Absolutely. OpenBosniana OS is designed with security and privacy as priorities, protecting users from viruses, malware, and other threats.


Customization and Support

Q: Can I customize OpenBosniana OS?

A: Yes, OpenBosniana OS is highly customizable. You can modify the desktop environment, themes, applications, and system settings to suit your preferences.

Q: Is OpenBosniana OS secure?

A: Absolutely. OpenBosniana OS is designed with security and privacy as priorities, protecting users from viruses, malware, and other threats.


Advanced Questions

Q: Can I use OpenBosniana OS for development?

A: Yes, the XFCE desktop environment is optimized for older or low-resource hardware, providing a lightweight yet functional experience.

Q: Is OpenBosniana OS suitable for education?

A: Yes, the EDUCATION edition of OpenBosniana OS includes specialized tools and applications tailored for students, teachers, and academic institutions.
